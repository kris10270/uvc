Inactivation vs. UV Dose

Dashed line shows the dose from the current plume transit.

Plume physics: For cough/sneeze: turbulent jet v(x) = V₀·(d₀/x), transit = (d²-d₀²)/(2·V₀·d₀). For breath/talking: transit ≈ d/V₀.
Survival: S(dose) = (1-f)·exp(-k1·dose) + f·exp(-k2·dose), dose in mJ/cm².
UV dose: irradiance(µW/cm²) × transit(s) / 1000 → mJ/cm².
Pathogen data: UVC Inactivation Constants from guv-calcs (Belenky). Plume velocities: Gupta 2009, Bourouiba 2014, Abkarian 2020.
